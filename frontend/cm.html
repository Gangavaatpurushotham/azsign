Super 6 Schemes

1. Monthly aid to women – ₹1,500/month pension assistance
2. Youth job support – 20 lakh jobs or ₹3,000/month allowance
3. Educational support – ₹15,000/year (Thalliki Vandanam)
4. Farmer assistance – ₹20,000/year (Annadata Sukhibhava)
5. Free LPG – 3 cylinders per year (Deepam-2)
6. Free transport – Bus travel for women in APSRTC

Eligibility & Documents

Eligibility:
Women of Andhra Pradesh traveling in APSRTC buses are eligible.
Documents Required:
Valid Aadhaar Card (Physical Copy – Offline only, not online version).
Note:
Passengers must carry the Aadhaar card while traveling to avail the free bus service.
My Opinion:
This scheme is really useful as it reduces travel costs and empowers women’s mobility.
